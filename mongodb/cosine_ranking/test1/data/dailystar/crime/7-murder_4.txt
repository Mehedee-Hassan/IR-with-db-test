Chief Justice Surendra Kumar Sinha has instructed the officials concerned of the High Court to quickly prepare the paper book of death reference in sensational Narayanganj seven-murder case.



The chief justice will assign a High Court bench to hear the death reference after the paper book is prepared, Additional Registrar of the HC Subbir Foez told reporters today.



The death reference of the case reached the HC on January 22.



A paper-book contains all the details of a case, trial proceedings, statements, evidence, verdicts and other documents. The book is necessary for the HC to hear and dispose of a death reference or an appeal.

On January 16, Narayanganj District and Sessions Judge's Court found 35 people, including 25 former Rab officials and expelled ruling AL man Nur Hossain, guilty of abduction and murder of seven people and destroying evidence in April 2014.



The court handed down death penalty to 26 people, including 16 former Rab men and jailed nine other accused for various terms, from seven to 17 years.
